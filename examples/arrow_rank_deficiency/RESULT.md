# Result

Headline finding:

- plurality, approval, and ranked choice appear as explicit observer maps on one
  latent preference geometry
- the hidden-load clock drops strictly as the observer becomes richer
- the hidden-load spectra identify which preference directions remain hidden
  under each voting rule
- richer-to-coarser descent composes exactly in the observer geometry, with
  residual `8.88e-16`
- direct determinant-gap agreement holds at `1e-15` scale
- the ranked-choice hidden load vanishes up to floating-point residual
