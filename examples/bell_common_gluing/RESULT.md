# Result

Headline finding:

- there is an open region in parameter space where the arcsine-CHSH correlator
  criterion passes but common Gaussian gluing still fails
- the obstruction in that region is variance consistency, not correlator
  nonlocality
- the same normalized correlator matrix appears in both compatible and
  incompatible families, so the separation is genuinely beyond correlators
- compatible sample points are reconstructed as a common latent Gaussian law and
  re-observed through `nomogeo` with pair-precision residual `5.55e-16`
- the variance-only sample has the same correlator matrix and same positive
  arcsine margin as the compatible sample, but linear completion residual
  `0.11`
- the confirmed variance-only neighborhood is the `5 x 5` grid on
  `delta in [0.18, 0.26]`, `rho in [0.56, 0.66]`
